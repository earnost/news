World

F-35 Sale Resuming

United States President Donald Trump announced that he plans to lift sanctions on Turkey and is open to resuming sale of F-35 fighter jets, reversing a ban that he imposed in his first term. Opposition has risen from the European Union, Isreal, and members of the U.S. Congress. Lawmakers argue that this move violates the 2020 National Defense Authorization Act. Some members of congress state that this move rewards Turkey's violent behaviour, Trump suggests that Turkey has proved to be a good ally and is deserving of the decision.

Iran Ceasefire Ends

The United States has begun strikes in Iran once again after another unsuccessful ceasefire. U.S. Central Command states that its goal in these strikes is to degrade Iran's ability to threaten commercial shipping in the Strait of Hormuz

National

SCOTUS ends Temporary Protected Status

In Mullin v. Doe, The Supreme Court ruled 6-3 that the Presidential administration can end Temporary Protected Status for refugees without judicial review. This immediately affects over 300,000 Hatians currently living in the United States that were granted TPS under president Barack Obama.
Many have falsely confused TPS with immigration. A refugee living under TPS may be a temporary legal resident, but is not an illegal immigrant, or an immigrant by any means. While it could be possible to be under TPS and then apply to immigrate as a citizen, most conflations of the two are incorrect.

Trump Accounts

"A new era of investing for America's youngest generation", stated President Trump. Children born between 2025 and 2028 will recieve an intial investment account of $1,000. The initial investment is funded by taxpayer dollars and private donations. Parents can also make additions to the account yearly. The money grows tax deffered until they're withdrawn from the account at 18 years of age. After 18, the account becomes a traditional IRA. This move generally had bipartisian support. Investment company Robinhood secured the management and infrastructure of these accounts.

Chokwe Lumumba pled guilty to a single count of conspiracy to commit bribery, wire fraud, and money laundering. Initially, Lumumba called the FBI sting "political prosecution" intended to harm his run for re-election.
Former Jackson city coucilmen Aaron Banks and Hinds County District Attourney Jody Owens have each submitted guilty pleas for this same scheme.

18-Year Old Found Dead

While on a Fourth of July boat trip to Horn Island off the Gulf Coast, Nolan Wells dissapeared. His body was found by a park ranger on July 7th. Authorities belive that Wells drowned after his friends returned to land without him. Jackson County Sheriff John Ledbetter stated that there is no current suspicion of foul play.
The Wells family has hired civil rights attourney Ben Crump, who is conducting an independent investigation.

Instagram's New AI Tools

Meta's Instagram rolled out new AI features this week, allowing anyone with an instagram account to create custom AI videos using any account's public instagram photos. Users will not be notified when their content is used by others for AI content. All public accounts will be default opted-in to this, while user can select the option to opt-out in their user settings.
